DES Engineering Services and Construction

DES is a company based in Frankfurt - Germany was emanated from DES a limited liability company established in 2002 in GErmany. The company is managed by professionally qualified international staff, who has an accumulated Engineering expertise of 40 years in construction industry and 20 years on the buisness side.

In order to maximize customer satisfaction, we ensure that the services we provide are of exceptionally high quality and that the entire operation is backed up with efficent service.

ABOUT US

DES Engineering Services and Construction specializes in Engineering, Procurement and Construction. We are one of the largest contractors in the region and have helped build some of the area’s most recognizable landmarks. Our team bring their technical knowledge, experience, and resourcefulness to the delivery of our construction services. Driven by a commitment to continuous improvement, DIS embraces Building Information Modeling and lean construction methods to improve efficiency and enhance client value.

Our firm is capable of stepping in at any stage of a development project, from planning and entitlements through construction, and successfully seeing it through to completion. Regardless of the service provided, our projects are all grounded in a strong sense of reality and understanding of what it takes to build a quality project globally.
